Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Click the "Sign Up" button on the homepage. Enter your username, email, and password. Your account is created instantly and secured with password hashing. You can start using Sahaay immediately!

Is my data secure?

Yes! We use bank-level encryption for all data. Passwords are hashed with industry-standard algorithms. All messages are stored securely in our encrypted PostgreSQL database. We never share your data with third parties.

How do I use the voice assistant?

Navigate to the "Assistant" page. Click the microphone button to start voice input. Speak clearly and the app will convert your voice to text automatically. You can also type messages manually. Your voice is converted to text and saved to your history.

What browsers are supported?

Sahaay works on all modern browsers: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, and Opera. Mobile browsers (iOS Safari, Chrome Mobile) are fully supported. Requires JavaScript enabled and a working microphone for voice features.

Can I access my messages on multiple devices?

Yes! Your entire message history is stored in the cloud. Log in from any device and see all your conversations. Changes sync instantly across all your devices. Your profile and settings are available everywhere.

How many languages does Sahaay support?

Sahaay supports 25+ languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Mandarin, Japanese, Hindi, and more. The app automatically detects your language preference. Text-to-speech is available in all supported languages with regional accent options.

Is Sahaay free to use?

Yes! Sahaay is completely free to use. No hidden charges, no premium tiers (for now). We provide unlimited voice input, message storage, and all features at no cost. This is our commitment to accessibility.

What if my microphone isn't working?

Check these steps: 1) Ensure your browser has microphone permission 2) Test your microphone in system settings 3) Refresh the page 4) Try a different browser. You can also type messages manually without using the microphone feature.

How do I reset my password?

On the login page, click "Forgot Password". Enter your email address and we'll send you a password reset link. Follow the link to create a new password. If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact support.

Troubleshooting Guide

🎤 Voice Not Working

  • Check browser microphone permissions
  • Test mic in system settings
  • Try another browser
  • Restart your computer
  • Email: support@sahaay.ai

🔐 Login Issues

  • Clear browser cache & cookies
  • Check caps lock (password is case-sensitive)
  • Reset your password
  • Try a different browser
  • Contact support if persists

⚡ Slow Performance

  • Check your internet connection
  • Close other browser tabs
  • Clear browser cache
  • Update your browser
  • Try WiFi instead of mobile data
